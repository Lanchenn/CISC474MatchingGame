Instruction!

The goal of the game is to pair the all cards inside the pattern

Simply by clicking two same cards will get scores

If you click on the different cards, both cards will flip over and you have to click it again.

-------------- Simple Mode -------------

When all the cards are paired, you WIN!

----------------------- Hard Mode ----------------------

Once you click the wrong pair, your game time decrease.

When all the cards are paired, you WIN!

Good Luck & Have Fun!
